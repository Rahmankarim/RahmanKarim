title: "Why I Started Writing About Code"
date: "2024-12-15"
---

Writing about code has changed the way I think about programming. What started as a way to document my learning has become a powerful tool for deepening my understanding.

## The Power of Explanation

When you have to explain a concept to others, you quickly discover what you actually understand versus what you merely think you understand. This gap is where real learning happens.

## Building a Public Archive

Over time, these posts become a personal archive of your journey. Years later, you can look back and see how your thinking has evolved, what problems you've solved, and how your perspective has shifted.

The act of writing forces clarity. It transforms fuzzy ideas into concrete thoughts that can be critiqued, refined, and built upon.
